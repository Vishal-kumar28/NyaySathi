Know Your Rights

As an Indian citizen, you are entitled to rights protected under the Constitution of India:

  • Right to Equality: Equal protection of the law for every citizen.
  • Right to Freedom: Freedom of speech, expression, and movement.
  • Right against Exploitation: Prohibition of trafficking and forced labor.
  • Right to Constitutional Remedies: Approach courts for enforcement of rights.
